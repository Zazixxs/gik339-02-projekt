Star Boys Fanclub

The Carlito Menace

In the ordinarily orderly galaxy, a menace stirs not from the dark corners of space but from the trendy streets of Stockholm...

Carlito, a quirky inventor from the chic planet of Stockhomeia, has unleashed his latest creation: the Glacial Groove, a device that sends waves of irresistible dance fever, turning the galaxy's disciplined marches into spontaneous ABBA tribute flash mobs.

Revenge of the Luddo

In the usually stoic and orderly galaxy, an unexpected character has caused a cosmic conundrum...

He is Luddo, the self-proclaimed "Youngling Slayer," but not in the way you'd imagine. Armed with an ancient yo-yo and a collection of classic board games, Luddo has declared a one-man war against the newest generation of tech-savvy beings, challenging them to battles of wits and skill rather than actual combat.

The Hugo Strikes Back

In the not-so-distant corner of the galaxy, the Hugo system is facing an unexpected menace...

The dark lord, Kimchi Vader, has emerged with a plan to conquer the galaxy using his ultimate weapon, the Fermenter Death Star, capable of turning entire planets into cosmic kimchi. The inhabitants of Hugo, known for their peaceful ways and love for interstellar BBQ parties, find themselves in a pickle.

Attack of the Olive

In a galaxy known for its complex politics and interstellar battles, a new, rather unconventional threat emerges: Olive, the teacher...

Not just any teacher, but one whose methods are as unconventional as they are legendary. Known across star systems for his unpredictable quizzes and penchant for pop-up history lessons, Olive has decided it's high time the galaxy got educated, whether it wants to or not
